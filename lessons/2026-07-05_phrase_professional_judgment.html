professional judgment

/prəˈfeʃənəl ˈdʒʌdʒmənt/
noun phrase
📖 Meaning

an expert opinion based on knowledge, experience, and responsibility

🧠 Usage Explanation

Use this when explaining why clients should listen to your recommendation. It is more mature than saying “my idea.”

🧱 Sentence Pattern
  • provide professional judgment
  • based on my professional judgment
  • clients pay us for professional judgment
💬 Examples
Clients pay us for our professional judgment, not only execution.
consulting
Based on my professional judgment, this option creates less compliance risk.
recommendation
Professional judgment means giving honest advice, even when the client prefers another option.
client communication
As an SA, I need to combine technical knowledge with professional judgment.
work identity
If we only follow instructions, we are not really using our professional judgment.
consulting mindset
🧩 Common Collocations
professional judgment
Use professional judgment in complex situations.
expert opinion
The client needs an expert opinion.
recommendation
My recommendation is based on your business requirements.
