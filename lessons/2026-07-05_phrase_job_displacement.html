job displacement

/dʒɑːb dɪsˈpleɪsmənt/
noun phrase
📖 Meaning

workers losing jobs because tasks are replaced by technology or economic change

🧠 Usage Explanation

Use this when discussing AI and employment. It is more precise and formal than “people don’t have jobs because of AI.”

🧱 Sentence Pattern
  • cause job displacement
  • face job displacement
  • reduce job displacement through reskilling
💬 Examples
AI may cause job displacement in some repetitive roles.
AI and society
Job displacement is one of the biggest concerns about automation.
social issue
Companies can reduce job displacement by helping workers reskill.
solution
The benefit of AI is productivity, but the risk is job displacement.
balanced view
Job displacement is not only a technical issue; it is also a social issue.
discussion
🧩 Common Collocations
job displacement
AI may create job displacement.
automation
Automation can replace repetitive tasks.
labor market
Job displacement affects the labor market.
