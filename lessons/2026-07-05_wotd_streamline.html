Word of the Day

streamline

/ˈstriːm.laɪn/
verb
📖 Definition

to make a process or system more efficient by simplifying or removing unnecessary steps

Streamline is a core business term. You streamline processes, workflows, operations — making them faster and smoother.

💬 Examples
We streamlined the deployment process from 12 weeks to 6.
AI can streamline inventory management in manufacturing.
The new system streamlines communication between sales and engineering.
🔗 Synonyms
simplifyoptimizesmoothaccelerate
complicateslow downburdenclog
🧩 Common Collocations
streamline + [process/operation/workflow]
streamline the onboarding process
streamline + [something] + to + [result]
streamline operations to reduce costs
