Speaking: OPC Budget Pushback

2026-07-22 · 20-30 min · Echo English for Lance
Speaking Scenario
🎯 Today's Goal 2 min

Practice responding when an OPC customer says the cloud solution is too expensive.

🧠 Scenario 4 min

An OPC customer has a small team and limited budget. They like Alibaba Cloud, but they worry about monthly cost and operation complexity.

💬 Dialogue Practice 10 min

Client: This solution looks good, but the monthly cost is higher than we expected.

Lance: I understand. For a small team, cost control is very important. We can start with a lean architecture and scale only when the traffic grows.

Client: Can we reduce the initial cost?

Lance: Yes. We can prioritize the must-have components first, then add monitoring and automation step by step.

Client: I do not want too much maintenance work.

Lance: In that case, I would recommend managed services where possible, so your team can focus on the business instead of infrastructure.

🧩 Natural Alternatives 6 min
Instead of: It is not expensive.
Say: The cost can be controlled if we start with a lean architecture.
Instead of: You need all of these services.
Say: We can prioritize the must-have components first.
Instead of: This is easy.
Say: Managed services can reduce the operational workload.
🎤 Output Task 8 min

Answer this customer objection in English:

"Your proposal is useful, but it is beyond our budget for now."

Use a calm structure: acknowledge - reframe - offer a smaller first step.
