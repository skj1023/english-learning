Vocabulary Upgrade
🎯 Today's Goal 2 min

Upgrade your vocabulary for healthcare cloud and AI discussions. Focus on expressions you can use in client meetings.

🔥 5 Expressions 15 min
regulatory requirements
监管要求。Example: We need to consider regulatory requirements before moving patient data to the cloud.
clinical workflow
临床工作流。Example: The AI tool should fit into the existing clinical workflow.
operational efficiency
运营效率。Example: The goal is to improve operational efficiency without increasing risk.
audit trail
审计追踪。Example: A clear audit trail helps the customer meet compliance requirements.
phased rollout
分阶段上线。Example: I recommend a phased rollout rather than a big-bang migration.
🧩 Sentence Patterns 6 min
  1. To meet regulatory requirements, we need to ______.
  2. This solution can improve operational efficiency by ______.
  3. A phased rollout would reduce the risk of ______.

Create 3 sentences for a healthcare customer using:
