Workplace English: SA Client Update

2026-07-08 · 20-30 min · Echo English for Lance
Workplace Communication
🎯 Today's Goal 2 min

Practice giving a clear SA-style project update to an OPC or healthcare client. By the end, you should be able to explain progress, risk, and next steps in 60 seconds.

📥 Input: Client Update Framework 6 min

1. Context: We reviewed your current cloud architecture and the main business requirements.

2. Progress: We have identified two suitable options for the pilot environment.

3. Risk: The key risk is healthcare data compliance, especially around access control and audit logs.

4. Next step: I suggest we define the pilot scope and confirm the success criteria this week.

🧩 Useful Expressions 8 min
Here is a quick update on where we are.
给客户做简短进展更新。
The main constraint is data compliance.
说明医疗客户最关键的限制条件。
We can start with a small, low-risk pilot.
适合 OPC 客户，预算和资源有限时使用。
The next step is to align on success criteria.
把讨论推进到可执行的下一步。
Does this direction match your priority?
确认客户是否认可方向。
🎤 Speaking Output 10 min

Speak for 60 seconds using this structure:

  1. Here is a quick update on...
  2. So far, we have...
  3. The main risk or constraint is...
  4. My suggestion is...
  5. Could you confirm whether...?
