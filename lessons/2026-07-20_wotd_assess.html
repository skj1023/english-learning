Word of the Day

assess

/əˈses/
verb
📖 Meaning

to carefully judge a situation, risk, need, or value

🧠 Usage Explanation

Use "assess" when you need to evaluate a customer requirement before proposing a solution. It sounds more professional than "check".

🧱 Sentence Pattern
  • assess the current setup
  • assess the risk of something
  • assess whether something is suitable
💬 Examples
We need to assess your current setup before recommending an architecture.
SA discovery call
The team should assess the security risk before migration.
cloud security
Can we assess whether this model is suitable for clinical use?
healthcare AI
I will assess the workload and suggest a phased plan.
project planning
It is too early to decide before we assess the business impact.
client meeting
🧩 Common Collocations
assess requirements
We should assess requirements before designing the solution.
assess risk
Healthcare projects need careful risk assessment.
assess suitability
We need to assess the suitability of the AI model.
