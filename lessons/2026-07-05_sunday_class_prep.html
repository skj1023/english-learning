"Hi, I'm Lance. I work as a Solutions Architect at Alibaba Cloud.

Today I'd like to practice talking about my work and AI solutions. Specifically:
1. Introducing my role naturally
2. Discussing how AI can help different types of clients
3. Using more precise business English expressions

Please feel free to correct my grammar and suggest more natural expressions. Thanks!"
❓ 课堂讨论问题
1. Can you describe what you do as a Solutions Architect in simple terms?
2. What kind of clients do you work with at Alibaba Cloud?
3. Can you give an example of an AI solution you recently worked on?
4. What are the biggest challenges when introducing AI to a client?
5. How do you explain technical concepts to non-technical clients?
6. What's the difference between a proof of concept and full deployment?
7. What AI trends are you most excited about right now?
8. How do you handle a client who is unsure about adopting AI?

"My role involves understanding client needs and designing solutions."

"We start with a proof of concept to demonstrate value."
"It's tailored to the client's specific needs."
"The key is to understand their pain points first."
"I bridge the gap between business challenges and technical solutions."
