🎤 Speaking Framework 8 min

Question: What was the biggest difference between studying in China and studying in Australia?

Compared with China, I would say Australian education was more career-oriented and discussion-based. In China, at least based on my experience, many courses were more textbook-based. Students were expected to remember what teachers taught. But in Australia, I had to do more research, work with peers, and form my own judgment. The most valuable thing I learned was critical thinking. It taught me not to accept information blindly, but to verify information and look at things from different angles.
