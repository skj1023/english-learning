align on the business goal

/əˈlaɪn ɑːn ðə ˈbɪznəs ɡoʊl/
phrase
📖 Meaning

to make sure everyone agrees on the main business objective

🧠 Usage Explanation

Use this before discussing solutions. It helps you sound structured and strategic, especially in client meetings.

🧱 Sentence Pattern
  • align on the business goal first
  • before we choose a solution, we should align on...
  • align the solution with the goal
💬 Examples
Before choosing a solution, we should align on the business goal.
meeting structure
If we do not align on the business goal, the technical discussion may go in the wrong direction.
risk
Let’s align on the business goal before we compare different options.
client meeting
The architecture should align with the client’s business goal.
solution design
Sometimes clients propose a solution before they clearly define the business goal.
consulting insight
🧩 Common Collocations
align on the goal
Let’s align on the goal first.
business goal
What business goal are we trying to achieve?
success criteria
Success criteria should match the business goal.
