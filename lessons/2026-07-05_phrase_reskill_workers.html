reskill workers

/ˌriːˈskɪl ˈwɜːrkərz/
verb phrase
📖 Meaning

to help workers learn new skills for different or changing jobs

🧠 Usage Explanation

Use this when talking about solutions to AI-related unemployment. It sounds more practical than simply saying “learn more.”

🧱 Sentence Pattern
  • reskill workers
  • help workers reskill
  • reskill themselves for new roles
💬 Examples
Governments and companies need to reskill workers.
AI policy
If AI replaces repetitive tasks, workers may need to reskill themselves.
employment
Reskilling workers is easier to say than to do, because training takes time and money.
realistic view
Companies should not only cut costs; they should also invest in reskilling workers.
business responsibility
A strong social safety net should include reskilling programs.
policy
🧩 Common Collocations
reskill workers
Companies should reskill workers for AI-era jobs.
reskilling program
A reskilling program helps people transition.
upskill
Upskill means improving current skills; reskill means learning new ones.
