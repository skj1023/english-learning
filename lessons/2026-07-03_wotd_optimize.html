Word of the Day

optimize

/ˈɒp.tɪ.maɪz/
verb
📖 Definition

to make something as effective, efficient, or functional as possible

You already have something working — now make it better, faster, or cheaper. It's about improvement, not creation.

💬 Examples
We helped the client optimize their supply chain using AI.
The system needs to be optimized for better performance.
Our team focuses on optimizing cloud infrastructure costs.
🔗 Synonyms

🧩 Common Collocations

optimize performance / optimize costs
