Word of the Day

align

/əˈlaɪn/
verb
📖 Definition

to bring things into agreement or coordination with each other

To align means to make sure different things are working toward the same goal. Used constantly in business for strategy, teams, and objectives.

💬 Examples
We need to align our AI strategy with the client's business goals.
The solution is aligned with industry best practices.
Let me align with the engineering team before we commit to this timeline.
🔗 Synonyms
coordinatesynchronizeharmonizereconcile
conflictdivergemismatchdisagree
🧩 Common Collocations
align + [something] + with + [something]
align the project timeline with the client's expectations
stay aligned
Let's stay aligned on next steps.
