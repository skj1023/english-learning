💬 Dialogue 8 min

Client: We want to use AI, but we do not have a large budget.

Lance: That makes sense. Could you clarify your most urgent business problem?

Client: We spend too much time answering repeated customer questions.

Lance: In that case, we can start with a focused pilot, such as an FAQ assistant. It keeps the scope small and helps you see value quickly.

Client: What about security?

Lance: We can design the solution with access control, logging, and clear data boundaries from the beginning.
