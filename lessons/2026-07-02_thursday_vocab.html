🌅 通勤预习 白天路上

记住一个原则：升级不是为了显得高级，是让表达更精确。

use
leverage 利用/发挥优势
"We leverage AI to improve decision-making."
help
enable 使能够
"Our solution enables clients to reduce costs by 30%."
make
drive 驱动/推动
"AI can drive better business outcomes."
find
identify 识别/判定
"We identify opportunities where AI adds value."
talk about
address / discuss 讨论 / 应对
"Let's address the timeline in today's meeting."
