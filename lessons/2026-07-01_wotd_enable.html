Word of the Day

enable

/ɪˈneɪ.bəl/
verb
📖 Definition

to make something possible or give someone the ability to do something

More powerful than 'help' — it means giving someone or something the capability to do something they couldn't do before.

💬 Examples
Our platform enables clients to reduce operational costs by 30%.
AI enables real-time decision-making across the organization.
This tool enables non-technical users to build their own dashboards.
🔗 Synonyms
empowerallowfacilitatemake possible
preventblockhinderdisable
🧩 Common Collocations
enable + [someone] + to + [verb]
enable clients to scale their operations
enable + [something]
enable real-time processing / enable seamless integration
