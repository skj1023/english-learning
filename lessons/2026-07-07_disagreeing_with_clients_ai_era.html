Key principle: A client is not always right, but they should always feel respected.

🎤 Speaking Framework A 6 min

Question: How do you disagree with a client professionally?

I don't think clients are always right, but they should always feel respected. If I disagree with a client, I won't reject their idea directly. I usually try to understand their perspective first. Then I explain my recommendation based on their business goal. Sometimes, I also put both options on the table and compare the pros and cons. In my view, clients pay us not only to follow instructions, but also to provide professional judgment.
🎤 Speaking Framework B 6 min

Question: Do you think AI will replace many jobs?

I think AI will replace some repetitive jobs, but it will also create new opportunities. For companies, AI can boost productivity and reduce operating costs. For example, a one-person company can use AI agents to handle tasks that used to require a team. However, the downside is job displacement. Many people may need to reskill themselves. In my view, AI is like Pandora's box. Once society opens it, we cannot simply go back. The real challenge is how to manage the risks.
