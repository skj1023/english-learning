🌅 通勤预习 白天路上

路上通勤时反复读以下内容，不用写，先熟悉。

🔑 今日核心表达（5个）

① work as — 担任……职位
"I work as a Solutions Architect at Alibaba Cloud."
② responsible for — 负责……
"I'm responsible for helping clients implement AI solutions."
③ involve — 包括……
"My role involves understanding customer needs and designing solutions."
④ bridge the gap between — 在……之间架桥
"I bridge the gap between business challenges and technical solutions."
⑤ add value — 创造价值
"I help clients identify where AI can add value to their business."

📖 自我介绍（通勤时反复读出声）

简短版（30秒）

"Hi, I'm Lance. I work as a Solutions Architect at Alibaba Cloud. I help clients find AI solutions that fit their business needs."

标准版（60秒）

"Hi everyone, I'm Lance from Alibaba Cloud. I work as a Solutions Architect, which means I help clients identify and implement AI solutions. My role involves understanding their business challenges and designing tailored solutions. I focus on areas where AI can add real value."

详细版（90秒）

"I'm Lance, a Solutions Architect at Alibaba Cloud. I work with healthcare and pharma companies to help them apply AI to real problems. One area I focus on is pharmacovigilance — using AI to detect adverse drug reactions from patient data. It speeds up what used to take manual effort and helps improve patient safety. That's the kind of impact I help deliver."
