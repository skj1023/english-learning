present solutions to clients

/prɪˈzent səˈluːʃənz tə ˈklaɪənts/
workplace phrase
📖 Meaning

to explain recommended options or products to customers clearly and persuasively

🧠 Usage Explanation

This phrase is useful for describing your SA work. It implies not only showing slides, but also explaining value, trade-offs, risks, and next steps.

🧱 Sentence Pattern
  • present a solution to a client
  • present solutions based on business requirements
  • walk the client through the proposed solution
💬 Examples
As an SA, I often present solutions to clients based on their business requirements.
work identity
When we present solutions to clients, we should explain the value, risk, and next steps.
meeting structure
It is important to present solutions to clients in simple business language.
communication
I try not to present too many options at once, because that can confuse the client.
presentation strategy
A strong SA can present a technical solution in a way that business stakeholders understand.
SA skill
