present solutions to clients

/prɪˈzent səˈluːʃənz tə ˈklaɪənts/
phrase
📖 Definition

to explain recommended options or products to customers in a clear and persuasive way

Use this when describing SA work, pre-sales discussions, and client meetings.

💬 Examples
