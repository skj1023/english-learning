bridge the gap between

/brɪdʒ ðə ɡæp bɪˈtwiːn/
phrase
📖 Definition

to connect two different sides, needs, or levels of understanding

Very useful for SA work: technology vs business, product vs customer, Chinese vs English.

💬 Examples
A good SA can bridge the gap between technical teams and business stakeholders.
SA role
AI agents may bridge the gap between small teams and big-company capabilities.
AI / OPC
Studying abroad helped me bridge the gap between Chinese and Western learning styles.
education
