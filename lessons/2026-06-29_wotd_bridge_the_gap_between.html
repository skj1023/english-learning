bridge the gap between

/brɪdʒ ðə ɡæp bɪˈtwiːn/
phrase
📖 Meaning

to connect two different sides, needs, or levels of understanding

🧠 Usage Explanation

This is a very useful expression for your role because an SA often connects business language with technical implementation. It sounds more natural than saying “connect different things.”

🧱 Sentence Pattern
  • bridge the gap between A and B
  • help bridge the gap between A and B
  • there is a gap between A and B
💬 Examples
A good SA can bridge the gap between technical teams and business stakeholders.
SA role
AI agents may bridge the gap between small teams and big-company capabilities.
AI / OPC
Studying abroad helped me bridge the gap between Chinese and Western learning styles.
education
Cloud consulting can bridge the gap between what clients want and what they can actually implement.
consulting
There is often a gap between a client’s idea and the real technical requirements.
client discovery
