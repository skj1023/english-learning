understand their perspective

/ˌʌndərˈstænd ðer pərˈspektɪv/
phrase
📖 Meaning

to see a situation from another person’s point of view

🧠 Usage Explanation

Use this before disagreeing. It makes your disagreement sound respectful and professional instead of direct or aggressive.

🧱 Sentence Pattern
  • try to understand their perspective first
  • from their perspective
  • I understand your perspective, but...
💬 Examples
I try to understand their perspective before I disagree.
client communication
From the client’s perspective, cost control may be more important than technical elegance.
business trade-off
If we understand their perspective, we can propose a better solution.
consulting
I understand your perspective, but I see a few risks in this approach.
polite disagreement
Good communication starts with understanding the other person’s perspective.
general communication
🧩 Common Collocations
understand someone’s perspective
Understand their perspective before giving advice.
from a client’s perspective
From a client’s perspective, ROI matters.
different perspective
Let me offer a different perspective.
