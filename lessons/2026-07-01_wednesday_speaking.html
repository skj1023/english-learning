🌅 通勤预习 白天路上

场景设定

你是一位 Alibaba Cloud 的 SA，正在和一家制造企业的技术负责人开初次会议。他们想了解 AI 如何帮助优化生产线效率。

💬 情景对话

You: "Thank you for meeting with me. I understand you're looking into how AI can improve your production line."

Client: "Yes. We have a lot of sensor data, but we're not sure how to use it."

You: "That's exactly where AI can help. We can use that data to build a predictive maintenance model — it predicts equipment failures before they happen."

Client: "How long does implementation take?"

You: "We typically start with a proof of concept — that takes about 4-6 weeks. Once we show value, we scale up."

🔑 今日表达

① look into — 研究 / 调研
正式表达"正在探索某个方向"
"We're looking into using AI for quality control."
② predictive maintenance — 预测性维护
SA 向制造/工业客户时的高频术语
"Predictive maintenance is one of AI's most common use cases in manufacturing."
③ proof of concept (PoC) — 概念验证
技术方案销售中最核心的词之一
"We start with a small proof of concept to demonstrate value."
④ pain points — 痛点
了解客户需求时的关键表达
"Let me understand your pain points before we talk about solutions."
⑤ scale up — 规模化
PoC 成功后的下一步
"Once the PoC is successful, we help you scale up."
