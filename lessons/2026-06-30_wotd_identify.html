Word of the Day

identify

/aɪˈden.tɪ.faɪ/
verb
📖 Definition

to recognize or establish what something is

To find out or point out exactly what something is. In business, you identify problems first, then solve them.

💬 Examples
Identify the client's pain points before proposing a solution.
We need to identify which AI use cases will deliver the most value.
Can you identify any gaps in our current approach?
🔗 Synonyms
pinpointrecognizedeterminedetect
overlookmissignore
🧩 Common Collocations
identify + [problem/opportunity/need]
identify customer needs / identify growth opportunities
identify + [something] + as + [noun]
identify AI as a key priority
difficult/easy to identify
The root cause was difficult to identify.
