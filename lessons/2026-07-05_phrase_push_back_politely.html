push back politely

/pʊʃ bæk pəˈlaɪtli/
phrase
📖 Meaning

to disagree or challenge an idea in a respectful way

🧠 Usage Explanation

This is one of your most useful client communication phrases. It means you do not simply say yes, but you also do not embarrass the client.

🧱 Sentence Pattern
  • push back politely on an idea
  • push back politely when...
  • need to push back politely
💬 Examples
Sometimes an SA needs to push back politely when a solution is risky.
SA work
If a client asks for something unrealistic, we should push back politely.
client expectation
Pushing back politely can protect the client from making a poor decision.
professional judgment
I would push back politely by asking questions rather than rejecting the idea directly.
communication method
It is possible to push back politely and still make the client feel respected.
relationship management
🧩 Common Collocations
push back politely
We need to push back politely.
challenge an idea
Challenge the idea, not the person.
raise a concern
I’d like to raise one concern.
