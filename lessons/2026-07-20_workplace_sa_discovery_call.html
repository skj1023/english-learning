Workplace: SA Discovery Call

2026-07-20 · 20-30 min · Echo English for Lance
Workplace Communication
🎯 Today's Goal 2 min

Practice opening a discovery call with an OPC or healthcare customer, then explain your role as a Solutions Architect clearly and professionally.

📥 Input 5 min
A good SA does not jump into the solution too early. First, clarify the business goal, current architecture, constraints, and decision process.
重点不是马上卖方案，而是先确认目标、现状、限制和决策方式。
🧩 Key Expressions 8 min
Before we talk about the solution, may I understand your current setup?
在聊方案前，我可以先了解一下你们当前架构吗？
What business outcome are you trying to achieve?
你们希望达成什么业务结果？
Are there any compliance or data residency requirements?
是否有合规或数据驻留要求？
I can suggest a phased approach to reduce migration risk.
我可以建议分阶段方式来降低迁移风险。
Let me summarize what I heard.
我来总结一下我理解到的信息。
💬 Mini Dialogue 7 min

Client: We want to move our healthcare application to the cloud, but we are worried about security.

Lance: That makes sense. Before we talk about the solution, may I understand your current setup and the type of data you handle?

Client: We store patient records and appointment data.

Lance: In that case, we should clarify compliance requirements, access control, and the migration timeline first.
