hive

/haɪv/
noun
📖 Definition

a structure where bees live; by extension, a very busy place full of activity

In daily English, a hive of activity means a place where many people are busy doing different things.

中文提示：蜂巢；也可指“繁忙景象 / 忙碌场所”。《Resident Evil / 生化危机》里的 The Hive 是地下蜂巢基地。

💬 Examples
The office became a hive of activity before the product launch.
— workplace: everyone is busy preparing
The airport was a hive of activity during the holiday rush.
— travel: crowded and busy
In Resident Evil, the Hive is the underground research facility.
— movie/game context
A beehive is an excellent example of teamwork and clear roles.
— literal meaning: where bees live
🔗 Related Words
beehive colony busy place hub
quiet place empty room
🧩 Common Collocations
a hive of activity
The meeting room was a hive of activity before the client demo.
beehive / hive mind
A hive mind means a group thinking almost as one.
The Hive
A proper noun used for fictional or branded places, such as The Hive in Resident Evil.
